||| Join In On Your Phone

Point your phone at the set — everyone plays at once.

1

Join the venue WiFi

The network the performer tells you. (Online show? Skip to step 3 and just scan the QR.)

2

Turn OFF mobile data

The #1 reason the page won't load. Cellular / 4G / 5G off — WiFi only.

3

Open the link or scan the QR

http://…:8081/ — type it in, or scan the code on screen.

4

Wait for green “CONNECTED”

The status in the top corner turns green when you're in.

5

Add to Home Screen (optional)

Share → Add to Home Screen for an app icon you can reopen instantly.

6

Play together

Drag the two XY pads, tap the transport buttons, move the sliders. Every phone's input blends into one sound.

?

Stuck on “reconnecting…”?

Just wait — it retries on its own. Re-check that WiFi is joined and mobile data is OFF (step 2).
